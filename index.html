💡 Key Insight & Recommendation

The perceived "AI Skepticism" is actually an illusion driven by a single YouTube outlier (yt4 at 42%). Without it, AI creators actually faced less average skepticism than human creators. Recommendation: Brands should confidently deploy AI influencers. Audiences have normalized their presence, especially on fast-paced visual platforms like Instagram.
